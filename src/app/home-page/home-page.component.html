Code Coach

Code Coach is an academic coaching programme between students in secondary schools, supervised by the schools.

Students with difficulties in a subject who want to make progress can ask for explanations and advice to other students in their school, who have mastered their subject and have acquired a good working method.

With Code-Coach everything is quick and easy, just register as a Coach or request a Coaching Session on this website, and it will organise a private coaching session in the school.

"It is action that creates motivation".

Become a Coach

To help others increase their potential.

If you feel strong in a subject and want to help other students, sign up as a Coach at your school, you will strenghten your knowledge in the subject, you will develop new skills and above all you will feel useful. Your commitments will be recognised and rewarded by your school.

Benefit from Coaching

To improve your academic results and to gain motivation.

If you have difficulties in a subject and you want to progress, make an appointment for private coaching, and benefit from the academic and practical experience of another student at your school, who understands your concrete needs. Your willingness to progress will be recognised by your school.

"In learning you will teach, and in teaching you will learn".
